Decades of Unrivaled Insight

Founded by Brendon Rudolph, RudiBudi Checkers has spent over 25 years strictly focused on the hospitality and travel industries. Unlike generalist research firms, we speak the language of luxury. We understand that in a 5-star environment, it is not enough to simply serve coffee; it is about the grace, timing, and emotional connection of the service.

From boutique hideaways in Budapest to sprawling resorts in the Maldives, our evaluators are seasoned travelers who match your guest profile perfectly. We empower leadership teams with the granular data needed to refine operations and elevate the guest experience.
